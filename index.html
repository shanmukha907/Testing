The Filmmaker

About
Shanmukha

Shanmukha is a filmmaker and visual artist based in Bengaluru, India — specialising in cinematography, VFX, direction, and editing. A storyteller by instinct and a technician by training.

His journey began with short films and documentaries as early as 2016 at CREO Valley School of Film and Television, Bengaluru — shooting stories that mattered with whatever gear was at hand. That same spirit carries into every frame today: intention over equipment, story over spectacle.

From corporate films for Devi Sea Foods and Tarantula, to student news bulletins, short films, 3D generalist work, and documentary filmmaking — Shanmukha brings a versatile, hands-on approach to every production.
